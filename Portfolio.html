EXL WELLNESS

The Exercise app is a specialized fitness and mobility tool tailored for individuals with disabilities, developed using programming languages such as Java, Python, or Node.js and hosted on AWS services including EC2, S3, Lambda, and others. Users can enjoy personalized workouts, guided exercises, and progress tracking features that ensure an easy and effective way to stay motivated and active. With its comprehensive features aimed at improving strength, flexibility, and overall health, the Exercise app is the perfect tool for anyone looking to achieve their fitness goals.

PETS-9

Pets-9 is an innovative DeFi platform built on the Solana blockchain, leveraging java to provide a range of services for pet owners. With Pets-9, users can buy, sell, and trade coins, tokenize their pet pictures, make donations to animal shelters, and even adopt pets. It's an ideal platform for pet enthusiasts who want to contribute to animal welfare and connect with a community of like-minded individuals. Pets-9 is an inventive solution that is revolutionizing the way people interact with their pets and support animal shelters.
